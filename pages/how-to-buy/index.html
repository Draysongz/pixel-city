How to buy?

Simple, Easy, and Safe!


Joining the Million Pixel INJ project is a breeze. Our process is designed to be clear and easy for everyone.

  • Step 1: Choose Your Pixels

    • Select the size of your pixel area. The smallest option is a 10x10 pixel square (equivalent to 100 pixels).
    • Remember, larger pixels mean a clearer display on the wall. We recommend choosing a 20x20 pixel square.
    • On the desktop, you can pick your exact spot on the wall. This feature will be available on mobile soon.

  • Step 2: Upload and Link

    • Upload your picture.
    • Add a clickable link to your website, social media, youtube or any other URL. It's like your digital business card!

  • Step 3: Contact Information

    • Provide an email or other contact method for any queries and to receive your order confirmation, including a unique order ID.
    • This step is also important for the Pixel wall lottery notifications.

  • Step 4: Make the Payment

    • Complete your purchase by following the simple payment instructions.
    • Ensure you input your wallet address correctly for order and payment tracking.

Your pixel art and link will be live on the wall within 24 hours after payment has been received.
