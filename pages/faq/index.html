Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I pick my spot on the pixel wall?
    Yes, you can choose your exact spot on the wall. This feature is currently available for desktop users and will soon be available for mobile devices too. You can also reach out to our team to reserve a specific location.

  • What's the minimum or maximum number of pixels I can buy?
    The minimum is 100 pixels (10x10). There's no maximum limit. For custom solutions, please contact our team.

  • Where can I find the pixel pricing?
    Pixel prices are displayed on the main page and detailed in the About section.

  • Will pixel prices change?
    Yes, prices will adjust based on how many pixels have been sold. Buying early means lower prices.

  • What picture format and size should I use?
    Choose a square or rectangular layout. We'll adjust the format and size to fit your chosen pixel area.

  • Can I change the size and layout during purchase?
    You can adjust sizes and layouts before finalizing your order by restarting the buying process.

  • What type of picture should I upload?
    Any high-quality picture works best.

  • What kinds of links can I submit?
    You can submit links to social media, YouTube, Twitch, NFT marketplaces, company websites, or any link that adheres to our content guidelines.

  • What picture formats are accepted?
    We accept JPG and PNG formats.

  • Can I purchase without going through checkout?
    While we recommend using our website's checkout, you can contact us for assistance or custom solutions.

  • Will I get an order confirmation?
    Yes, an email confirmation with a unique order ID will be sent after purchase.

  • How long before my pixel appears on the wall?
    Updates usually happen within 24 hours, subject to weekends and time zone differences.

  • Can I sell or trade my pixel spot?
    This functionality will be available once the marketplace is launched. Until then, contact our team for ownership transfers.

  • How big is a square on the wall?
    Each square is 100 pixels (10x10). Pricing is based on the current pixel price multiplied by 100.
